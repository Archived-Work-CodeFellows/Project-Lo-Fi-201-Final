Submission Rules

  • Submissions are always open.
  • You may submit a maximum of 1 SONG per day.
  • SONG submissions should be an mp3 and/or ogg.
  • SONGS are reviewed weekly by our staff.
  • DO NOT submit the same SONG within the same weekly cycle. Duplicate content will be deleted by our system.
  • Any attempts to SPAM our system will result in automatic disqualification and banned profiles.
